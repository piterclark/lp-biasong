코리아 데스크

핀헤이로 네토(Pinheiro Neto)는 한국을 비롯한 아시아 여러 국가의 기업들을 상대로 50년 이상 전문적인 법률서비스를 제공하고 있습니다.

특히 금융, 자동차, 전자, 통신, 제약, 의료, 중공업, 화학, 건설, 인프라스트럭처, 소비재 등 다양한 분야의 한국 기업들에게 법률자문을 제공하고 있습니다.

또한 당사의 해당 전문팀과 협업을 통해 종합적인 접근법으로 업무를 진행하여 각 고객들의 특정 요구사항들을 충족시켜 드리고 있으며, 이와 더불어 한국내 유수한 로펌들과 긴밀한 협력 관계를 구축하고 있습니다.

여러 분야의 업무에 대한 풍부한 경험과 축적된 지식을 보유하고 있으며 저희 한국 고객들이 브라질 현지에서 지향하는 목표를 이룰수 있도록 일상적인 업무부터 복합적인 거래까지 최선의 지원을 제공해 드리고 있습니다.

저희 로펌 코리아 데스크 전문팀은 한국내에서 최상위 로펌에 근무한 경력을 보유하고 유창한 한국어 실력 및 한국 기업문화에 대해 정통한 지식을 지니고 있는 변호사들로 구성되어 있습니다.

Recognitions of the firm

Know our main highlights in the major national and international publications.

Antitrust Writing Awards 2025

On April 1, we were honored in two categories at the 2025 Antitrust Writing Awards: “Business Articles – Americas” and “Business Articles – General Antitrust”.

In the first category, the winning article was \"Non-Compete Clauses: When Antitrust Meets Labor Law in Brazil and the USA\", written by counsel Manuela Mendes Prata and Amanda Athayde.

In the second category, the winning article was \"Non-Monetary Sanctions in Antitrust: Paths to Effectiveness\", written by counsel Amanda Athayde and associate Giovana Vieira Porto.

Chambers Brazil Awards 2025

We were awarded law firm of the year in Data Protection, Environment and Tax by the publication Chambers and Partners.

The publication reviews achievements over the past 12 months, reflecting excellence in service and strategic growth.

Chambers Brazil: Contentious 2025

We are leaders in the Chambers Brazil: Contentious 2025 Guide, with the highest number of Band 1 rankings, totaling six. In addition, we lead the ranking in both individual recognitions (36) and number of lawyers in top positions (six).

Chambers Brazil: Industries and Sectors 2025

Pinheiro Neto Advogados has once again been featured in Chambers Brazil: Industries & Sectors, and Regions 2025. We were recognized in seven Band 1 categories in Chambers Brazil: Industries & Sectors, including a standout debut in the newly introduced Gambling & Betting ranking, where we were ranked Band 1. In the individual rankings, we highlight partner André Santa Ritta, ranked Band 1, and associate Gabriel Carvalho, recognized as Associate to Watch – both in the Gambling & Betting category.

Chambers Brazil: Regions 2025

In Chambers Brazil: Regions 2025, our Rio de Janeiro office maintained its Band 1 ranking in Tax, with nine members of our team being individually ranked.

Chambers Global 2025

The  Chambers Global 2025 guide recognized Pinheiro Neto Advogados as the Brazilian law firm with the highest number of ranked practices (28), many of which are classified in Band 1: Banking & Finance, Capital Markets: Debt, Capital Markets: Equity, Corporate/M&A: The Elite, Dispute Resolution: Litigation: Elite, Energy & Natural Resources: Mining, International & Cross-Border Capabilities, International Trade/WTO, Investment Funds, Tax Litigation: The Elite and Tax Non-contentious: The Elite.

As for individual recognitions, we have 44 distinguished lawyers - one in the Star Individual category, two as Senior Statespeople, and seven in Band 1.

Chambers High Net Worth 2025

For the fourth consecutive year, we have been recognized in the Private Wealth ranking of the Chambers High Net Worth 2025 guide, reflecting the excellence of our advisory services to high-net-worth individuals and families. This year, we were also ranked for the first time in the Family/Matrimonial category. Our partner Pythagoras Carvalho maintained his prominent position, while our counsel Priscila Stela Mariano da Silva made her debut in the guide.

Client Choice Awards 2025

Our partners Raphael Palmieri Salomão and Tatiana Guazzelli were recognized for the excellent client care they provide and the quality of their service. Raphael was awarded in the Banking and Financial Transactions category, while Tatiana was recognized in the Fintech & Blockchain in Brazil and Blockchain in Brazil categories. The awards were granted by Client Choice, a Law Business Research Group publication in the UK.

GAR 100 2025

Once again, we have been recognized as one of the world's leading arbitration firms, according to the GAR 100 2025 list published by the British publication Global Arbitration Review. The survey selects firms that stand out for their consistent experience in international arbitration. Partners Gilberto Giusti, Rodrigo Grion, Celso Mori, Thaís Chebatt, Rodrigo Carneiro, consultant Gustavo Carneiro de Albuquerque, and associates Thiago Del Pozzo Zanelato and Bruna Anita Teruchkin Felberg were recognized for their work.

GCR - Women in Antitrust 2025

Pinheiro Neto Advogados is once again represented in the Global Competition Review – Women in Antitrust 2025 guide. Partner Cristianne Zarzur has been recognized once more for her outstanding work, and this year, partner Amanda Athayde joins the guide for the first time.

GCR Awards 2025

Our firm was awarded the “Bunge/Viterra” case of the year in the “Merger Control Matter of the Year – Americas” category. Partner Leonardo Rocha e Silva and associate Alexandre Horn Pureza Oliveira participated in the case.

IFLR Americas Awards 2025

We were recognized as Brazil law firm of the year in the “Capital Markets” category and acknowledged for our expertise in Digital Finance at the IFLR Americas Awards 2025.

The “Light Group” case was also distinguished in the “Restructuring Deal of the Year” category.

Latin Lawyer Deal of the Year Awards 2025

We are proud to share that Pinheiro Neto Advogados was recognized by Latin Lawyer in the “Diversity Initiative Award” category for our internal project “PapodeHomem.\" In addition, two cases in which we were involved were recognized at the “Deal of the Year Awards 2025”:

  • Capital markets: SABESP completes largest LatAm privatisation deal of 2024.

  • Restructuring: Americanas gets Chapter 15 approval for restructuring plan.

The Latin American Lawyer Women Awards 2025

Two of our partners were honored in The Latin American Lawyer Women Awards 2025, which celebrates women lawyers who stand out in their respective fields. Angela Kung was named Lawyer of the Year – Pharma, Healthcare & Life Sciences, whereas Cristianne Zarzur was recognized as Lawyer of the Year – Competition & Antitrust.

The Legal 500 Brazil Awards 2025

We received six awards in The Legal 500 Brazil Awards 2025. Pinheiro Neto Advogados was recognized as firm of the year in Competition and Antitrust, Environment, and Dispute Resolution,also earning two individual recognitions:

  • Capital Markets Lawyer of the Year: Guilherme Sampaio Monteiro

  • Public Law Lawyer of the Year: Ricardo Levy

In addition, one of the deals we advised on was awarded:

  • Deal of the Year: Samarco Mineração Restructuring

Uqbar 2025

We led the market in number of Agribusiness Receivables Certificates (CRA) transactions and ranked first in total issuance volume. In the Credit Rights Investment Funds (FIDC) segment, we ranked third both in issuance volume and in number of transactions. We were also recognized for our performance in the Real Estate Receivables Certificates (CRI) market, in terms of both volume and transaction count. The rankings are based on transactions carried out in 2024. The Uqbar guide highlights key players across Brazil’s main structured finance markets.

Women in Business Law Awards 2025

The awards recognized the outstanding work in 2024 of our partners Camila Martino Parise in the category “Latin America Life Sciences Lawyer of the Year”, and Tatiana Guazzelli in the category “Latin America Outstanding Achievement Award”.

Women in Investigations 2025

Our partner Natalia Lugero de Almeida was recognized in the publication Global Investigations Review, which highlights 100 remarkable women in the white-collar crime investigations world.
